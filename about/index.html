I'm a seasoned financial and data journalist and academic researcher from the Philippines.
I am pursuing my masters of science degree in data journalism from Columbia Journalism School in New York, and scheduled to complete my program in August 2022.
Prior to Columbia, I was most recently business news head at Philstar.com in Manila and Philippines researcher for the Financial Times before that.
In 2016, I was awarded Finance Reporter of the Year by the Economic Journalists Association of the Philippines, the official umbrella group of economic reporters in the country, for my coverage of public finance.
I hold a masters degree in public administration from the Catholic University of Korea, and a bachelor's in journalism from University of Santo Tomas in Manila.

Data analysis: Excel, Postgres/SQL, Python: pandas, OCR, Camelot, bs4, selenium
Data visualization: Adobe Illustrator, QGIS, HTML, CSS, opensource interactive chart tools (datawrapper, rawgraphs, etc.), ai2html, d3.js
