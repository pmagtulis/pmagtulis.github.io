Duterte sets record in conflict killings due to siege in South

February 5, 2022
I analyzed annual data on conflicts released by the UN Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs. Data show the 2017 terrorist siege in Lanao del Sur, south of the Philippines made that year the deadliest in recent history in terms of conflict.
