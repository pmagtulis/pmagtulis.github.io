Here are the vote-rich areas for the 2022 Philippine polls

February 13, 2022

Come May 9, Filipinos are trooping to poll sites for their national elections where they are to elect President Rodrigo Duterte's successor as well as other national officials.
Below is a map showing the number of registered voters for this year's elections according to municipal and city location. Data is from the Commission on Elections and was compiled here.
There are over 65 million voters, excluding around 1 million overseas voters, for the 2022 polls.
